Mentorship Philosophy

My approach to mentoring students is based on fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and independent research skills. I believe in providing guidance and support while encouraging students to develop their own research ideas and methodologies.

I work closely with my students to help them identify research questions that align with their interests and career goals, and provide them with the necessary resources and support to pursue their research. I emphasize the importance of rigorous scientific methods, ethical research practices, and effective communication of research findings.

I am committed to creating an inclusive and supportive research environment where students from diverse backgrounds can thrive and contribute to advancing knowledge in geosciences. I encourage collaboration, peer learning, and participation in scientific conferences and workshops to broaden students' perspectives and build their professional networks.

My goal is to prepare students for successful careers in academia, industry, or government by helping them develop not only technical skills but also critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills that are essential for their professional success.

Research Opportunities

I am always looking for motivated and talented students to join my research group. If you are interested in pursuing research in marine geophysics, climate change, natural hazards, or related fields, please consider the following opportunities:

Ph.D. Positions

I am currently accepting applications for Ph.D. students interested in the following research areas:

  • Climate change impacts on coastal systems
  • Seismic hazard assessment using advanced geophysical methods
  • Groundwater quality and management in coastal areas
  • Geothermal energy exploration and development

Prospective Ph.D. students should have a strong background in geophysics, earth sciences, or related fields, and a keen interest in conducting independent research.

Apply Now

Masters Projects

I offer research projects for M.Tech. and M.Sc. students in various areas related to geophysics, climate change, and natural hazards. These projects provide students with hands-on experience in field work, data analysis, and research methodology.

Current project opportunities include:

  • Application of machine learning in geophysical data interpretation
  • Remote sensing for coastal vulnerability assessment
  • Geophysical investigation of groundwater resources
  • Seismic microzonation of urban areas
Apply Now

Undergraduate Research

I encourage undergraduate students to get involved in research through summer internships, final year projects, and research assistantships. These opportunities provide students with valuable research experience and help them develop skills that are essential for graduate studies and professional careers.

Undergraduate research opportunities are available in various areas, including:

  • Development of software tools for geophysical data analysis
  • Field surveys and data collection
  • Laboratory experiments and measurements
  • Data visualization and interpretation

Application Process

1

Review Research Areas

Review my research areas and publications to ensure that your research interests align with the work being done in my laboratory.

2

Prepare Application Materials

Prepare your application materials, including your CV, academic transcripts, statement of research interests, and contact information for references.

3

Contact Me

Send me an email with your application materials and a brief introduction. Please include "Research Opportunity Application" in the subject line.

4

Interview

If your application is shortlisted, you will be invited for an interview to discuss your research interests, background, and potential projects.

5

Admission and Funding

If selected, you will receive information about admission procedures and potential funding opportunities for your research.

Note: For Ph.D. and M.Tech. admissions, you must also apply through the official IIT Kharagpur admission process. Please visit the IIT Kharagpur Admission Portal for more information.
