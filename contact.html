Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply to join your research group?

+

To apply to join my research group, please send me an email with your CV, academic transcripts, and a brief statement of your research interests. For Ph.D. and M.Tech. admissions, you must also apply through the official IIT Kharagpur admission process. Please visit the IIT Kharagpur Admission Portal for more information.

Are there any funding opportunities available for research?

+

Yes, there are various funding opportunities available for research, including institutional fellowships, government scholarships, and project-specific funding. Ph.D. students are typically supported through institutional fellowships or research project funding. For specific information about funding opportunities, please contact me directly.

Can I visit your laboratory for a short-term research internship?

+

Yes, I welcome students for short-term research internships, typically during the summer or winter breaks. If you are interested in a research internship, please send me an email with your CV, academic transcripts, and a brief statement of your research interests at least 3 months before your intended internship period.

How can I collaborate with your laboratory for research projects?

+

I am open to research collaborations with academic institutions, research organizations, and industry partners. If you are interested in collaborating with our laboratory, please contact me with details of your research interests and proposed collaboration. We can discuss potential collaborative projects, funding opportunities, and resource sharing.

Do you offer consultancy services for industry projects?

+

Yes, I offer consultancy services for industry projects related to my areas of expertise, including geophysical surveys, environmental impact assessment, natural hazard assessment, and resource exploration. If you are interested in consultancy services, please contact me with details of your project requirements.
