Research

Exploring the impacts of climate change and sustainability

Research Areas

Climate Change Impact Assessment

Our research focuses on understanding the impacts of climate change on coastal areas, including sea level rise, coastal erosion, and changes in marine ecosystems. We use integrated approaches combining field observations, remote sensing, and numerical modeling to assess vulnerability and develop adaptation strategies.

Learn More

Seismic Hazard Assessment

We investigate seismic hazards through geophysical surveys, seismic monitoring, and advanced data analysis. Our research aims to improve understanding of earthquake mechanisms, develop better early warning systems, and enhance building codes and infrastructure resilience in seismically active regions.

Learn More

Groundwater Quality and Management

Our groundwater research addresses challenges related to water quality, saltwater intrusion in coastal aquifers, and sustainable management of groundwater resources. We employ geophysical methods, hydrogeological modeling, and water quality monitoring to develop effective management strategies.

Learn More

Geothermal Energy Exploration

We explore geothermal energy resources as sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels. Our research involves geophysical exploration, thermal modeling, and assessment of geothermal potential in various geological settings. We aim to develop efficient and environmentally friendly geothermal energy extraction methods.

Learn More

Research Methodology

Field Investigations

We conduct extensive field surveys using state-of-the-art geophysical instruments to collect high-quality data for analysis and interpretation.

Remote Sensing

We utilize satellite imagery and remote sensing techniques to monitor environmental changes and assess natural hazards over large areas.

Numerical Modeling

We develop and apply numerical models to simulate complex geological and geophysical processes and predict future scenarios.

Machine Learning

We employ advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze large datasets and extract meaningful patterns and insights.

Current Projects

Assessment of Coastal Vulnerability to Sea Level Rise in the Bay of Bengal

This project aims to assess the vulnerability of coastal communities to sea level rise and develop adaptation strategies to mitigate the impacts.

Funding: Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India

Duration: 2023-2026

Geophysical Investigation of Groundwater Resources in Drought-Prone Regions

This project focuses on mapping groundwater resources in drought-prone regions using integrated geophysical techniques to support sustainable water management.

Funding: Department of Science and Technology, Government of India

Duration: 2024-2027

Seismic Microzonation of Urban Areas for Earthquake Risk Reduction

This project involves detailed seismic hazard assessment and microzonation of urban areas to support earthquake-resistant urban planning and infrastructure development.

Funding: National Disaster Management Authority

Duration: 2022-2025
