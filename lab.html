Marine Resources and Natural Hazards Laboratory

State-of-the-art facilities for geophysical research

Laboratory Overview

The Marine Resources and Natural Hazards Laboratory at IIT Kharagpur is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for conducting cutting-edge research in marine geophysics, climate change, and natural hazards. Our laboratory provides a collaborative environment for researchers, students, and industry partners to work together on addressing complex challenges related to environmental sustainability and natural resource management.

The laboratory is divided into several specialized sections, including geophysical instrumentation, data processing and analysis, numerical modeling, and field equipment. We have a team of dedicated researchers and technical staff who support the laboratory operations and assist in research activities.

Our laboratory is committed to advancing scientific knowledge and developing innovative solutions for sustainable development and natural hazard mitigation through interdisciplinary research and collaboration.

Laboratory Facilities

Geophysical Instrumentation

Our laboratory is equipped with a wide range of geophysical instruments for field and laboratory measurements, including:

  • Geodetic grade GPS receivers and antennae
  • Very Low Frequency - EM equipment for subsurface resistivity estimation
  • Broadband seismometers for seismic monitoring
  • Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) for shallow subsurface imaging
  • Electrical Resistivity Tomography (ERT) system
  • Magnetometers for magnetic field measurements

Data Processing and Analysis

Our data processing and analysis facility includes:

  • High-performance computing cluster for data processing and numerical modeling
  • Specialized software for geophysical data processing and interpretation
  • GIS and remote sensing software for spatial data analysis
  • Machine learning and data analytics tools
  • Visualization systems for 2D and 3D data representation

Field Equipment

Our field equipment includes:

  • Portable water quality monitoring systems
  • Field sampling equipment for soil, water, and sediment collection
  • Weather stations for meteorological data collection
  • Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) for aerial surveys and mapping
  • Field vehicles and boats for field campaigns

Environmental Testing

Our environmental testing facility includes:

  • Water quality analysis equipment
  • Soil and sediment analysis instruments
  • Microscopy systems for sample examination
  • Sample preparation equipment
  • Environmental chambers for controlled experiments

Research Areas

Climate Change Impact Assessment

Research on the impacts of climate change on coastal areas, including sea level rise, coastal erosion, and changes in marine ecosystems.

Seismic Hazard Assessment

Investigation of seismic hazards through geophysical surveys, seismic monitoring, and advanced data analysis.

Groundwater Quality and Management

Research on groundwater quality, saltwater intrusion in coastal aquifers, and sustainable management of groundwater resources.

Geothermal Energy Exploration

Exploration of geothermal energy resources as sustainable alternatives to fossil fuels.

Remote Sensing Applications

Application of remote sensing techniques for environmental monitoring, natural hazard assessment, and resource exploration.

Machine Learning in Geosciences

Development and application of machine learning algorithms for solving complex geoscience problems.

Laboratory Access and Collaboration

Our laboratory is open for collaboration with academic institutions, research organizations, industry partners, and government agencies. We welcome researchers and students interested in using our facilities for their research projects.

Access for Students

Students enrolled in courses related to geophysics, earth sciences, and environmental studies can access the laboratory for course-related projects and assignments. Graduate students conducting research in relevant areas can apply for laboratory access through their academic advisors.

Research Collaboration

We are interested in collaborative research projects with academic institutions, research organizations, and industry partners. If you are interested in collaborating with our laboratory, please contact us with details of your research interests and proposed collaboration.

Industry Partnership

We offer laboratory services and expertise to industry partners for specific projects and consultancy. Our laboratory can provide technical support, data analysis, and expert advice for industry projects related to our areas of expertise.
